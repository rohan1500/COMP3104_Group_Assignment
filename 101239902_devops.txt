COMP3104 DevOps:

DevOps is a a term collision from two major trends; software development, and information technology operations. It enables businesses to deliver applications at a faster rate and bring together development and operations teams so there are fewer redundancies in the software development process. 

It has a set of processes that when applied correctly, it can drastically reduce IT overhead, remove manual tasks both of a mundane or complex nature, enforce the best security practices and more.
