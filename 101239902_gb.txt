George Brown College:

George Brown College is located in Toronto, Ontario. It has 3 main campuses; Casa Loma, St. James, and Waterfront. 

The schools offers many differnt types of programs, from information technology, business, health sciences, engineering etc. George Brown has a residence called "The George" which is located conveniently in downtown Toronto close to subway stops making it easy for students to go to any campus where they are studying.

George Brown offers hands-on, real-world experiences to make the best out of its students, preparing them for their future careers by simulating a real work environment. This ensures the skills you learn in class, are the ones you'll need on the job.
